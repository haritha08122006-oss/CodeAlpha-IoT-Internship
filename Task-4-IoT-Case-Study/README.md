Task 4 – IoT Case Study

CASE STUDY TITLE:
Future of IoT in Smart Cities

OBJECTIVE
To study the role of the Internet of Things (IoT) in the development of smart cities by exploring key technologies, real-world applications, challenges, and future trends.

DESCRIPTION
This case study examines how IoT is transforming modern cities through intelligent infrastructure, smart transportation, energy management, environmental monitoring, and public services. It also presents the Singapore Smart Nation initiative as a real-world example of successful IoT implementation and discusses emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Edge Computing, Digital Twins, and 6G.
